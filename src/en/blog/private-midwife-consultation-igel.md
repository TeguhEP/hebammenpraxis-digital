Germany's statutory health insurance (GKV) covers a wide range of midwife services. But there are moments during pregnancy, birth preparation, and the postnatal period when you need something more: more time, more depth, more flexibility — or simply someone who speaks your language and understands where you're coming from.

That's where IGeL comes in.

## What Is IGeL?

IGeL stands for **Individuelle Gesundheitsleistungen** — Individual Health Services. These are services that fall outside the coverage of statutory health insurance (GKV) and are paid for privately by the patient.

This doesn't mean they're inferior. It means they're beyond the standard package — either because they're more personalised than what GKV covers, because they fall outside the covered time period, or because they address needs that aren't included in the defined benefit catalogue.

> **Your rights:** Before I provide any IGeL service, I am required to inform you clearly about the costs and obtain your explicit consent. You are always free to decline — with no impact whatsoever on your standard covered care.

## What Private Services Do I Offer?

### Individual Consultation (1:1 Video Session)
Sometimes you have questions that deserve more than five minutes. In a one-on-one video consultation, we have real time — to go through your concerns properly, in English, at a pace that works for you.

This may be useful if you:
- have specific questions about pregnancy, birth preparation, or postnatal recovery that weren't fully addressed in regular appointments
- want support beyond what GKV covers
- value continuity — speaking with someone who already knows your situation

For expats especially, having a midwife who understands both the German system and the experience of navigating it from the outside can make a genuine difference.

### Group Consultation (Online Small Group Session)
Pregnancy and new parenthood can feel isolating — especially when your usual support network is in another country. In small online group sessions, you'll meet other families going through similar experiences.

Together we discuss topics around pregnancy, birth, and early parenthood in an open, supportive environment. Sometimes what helps most isn't just information — it's knowing you're not alone.

## Who Might Benefit?

Private consultations aren't for everyone — and they're never a replacement for the services your insurer covers. But they may be a good fit if you:

- want more personalised support and dedicated time
- need flexible scheduling that fits around work or childcare
- are an expat looking for English-language midwifery care
- want to continue support after your GKV coverage period ends
