I am Raisa Shofia — a qualified midwife with over ten years of experience in public and private hospitals across Germany. Since graduating from Hochschule Ludwigshafen in 2015, I have supported many families through pregnancy, birth, and the early weeks with their newborn.

In my day-to-day practice, I see how difficult it can be for many women to find a midwife — not because there are too few of us, but because distance, limited mobility, and long waiting times create real obstacles. Access to professional midwife care should not depend on where you live or how easily you can travel.

That is why I founded Hebammenpraxis-Digital — a practice that meets you where you are: via video, by phone, or with a home visit. No lengthy waiting periods. No compromise on the quality of care.

Why Hebammenpraxis-Digital

Care that fits your life — not the other way around.

Having a baby in a country that isn't your home comes with a particular kind of complexity. The language, the healthcare system, the paperwork, the distance from family — it adds up. Hebammenpraxis-Digital was built with this in mind. We offer professional midwifery care that is flexible enough to meet you where you are, clear enough to cut through the confusion, and personal enough to make you feel genuinely supported — from the first trimester to the final weeks of the postnatal period.

Flexible appointments

Home visits or video consultations — your choice, every time. No commuting across the city with a newborn, no waiting rooms, no rigid clinic hours. Video appointments connect us within minutes from wherever you are, and home visits bring care directly to you when in-person assessment is needed. Your comfort and your schedule come first.

Insurance-covered & private services

Many midwifery services — including pregnancy check-ins, birth preparation, postnatal care, and breastfeeding support — are covered by German statutory health insurance (GKV) under § 134a SGB V. In addition, private consultations (IGeL) are available for those who wish to go beyond the standard coverage. You decide what level of support is right for you.

One midwife, start to finish

The same midwife accompanies you from early pregnancy through the postnatal period. No handovers between staff, no repeating your history to someone new, no starting over. We believe that trust is built through consistency — and that the quality of care depends not just on knowledge and skill, but on truly knowing the person in front of you.
