Private Consultations (IGeL)

Self-pay midwifery services via video — expert, personalised guidance available from wherever you are in Germany or abroad.

Please note: All services on this page are Individual Health Services (Individuelle Gesundheitsleistungen / IGeL). They are not covered by statutory health insurance (Krankenkasse) and are charged as a private service. A private invoice (Privatrechnung) will be issued after each session.
🤱 Breastfeeding

Stillberatung intensiv

How is this different from the Stillbetreuung covered by my Krankenkasse? Your Krankenkasse covers Stillbetreuung as part of standard postnatal midwife care (§134a SGB V) — this includes a limited number of home visits with basic breastfeeding support. This service is different: it is a dedicated 60-minute in-depth video consultation for complex or persistent breastfeeding challenges. It is not included in GKV coverage and is billed privately.

An intensive one-on-one video consultation focused on resolving complex breastfeeding difficulties. We assess your situation thoroughly and develop a personalised action plan together.

Ideal for:
  • Persistent latch problems or pain during breastfeeding
  • Concerns about low or oversupply of milk
  • Engorgement, blocked ducts, or mastitis prevention
  • Breastfeeding after returning to work
  • When your GKV-covered Stillbetreuung visits have been exhausted

🥣 Complementary Feeding

Beikostberatung

Personalised guidance on introducing solid foods (Beikost) to your baby. We discuss the right timing, safe first foods, progression of textures, and how to support your baby's healthy relationship with food from the very start.

Ideal for:
  • Starting solids for the first time (around 4–6 months)
  • Baby-led weaning (BLW) questions
  • Concerns about food allergies or intolerances
  • Texture progression and building a varied diet
  • Picky eating or refusal of solid foods

🥗 Nutrition in Pregnancy

Ernährungsberatung in der Schwangerschaft

Individual nutrition counselling during pregnancy via video. We review your current diet, identify any gaps or concerns, and develop practical, realistic recommendations to support a healthy pregnancy for both you and your baby.

Ideal for:
  • Nausea, food aversions, or significant appetite changes
